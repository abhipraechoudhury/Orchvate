About Us

Orchvate is a confluence of Orchestra and Innovate. Orchvate offers innovative approach that will build an autism inclusive society, where boundaries are broken and we work together to create wonders. At Orchvate we are Psychologists specialized in Abnormal (Clinical) and Organizational Psychology. We have the professional expertise of dealing with individuals on the spectrum, understand their abilities, match them to the right roles and integrate them into the workplace because we understand the challenges they are likely to face. Alongside, we have the professional expertise to work with corporates, understand their requirements and partner to achieve the organizational goals.

Vision

Our Vision is to Build an Autism Inclusive Society in India. We envision corporate driven community change as one of the primary drivers for a powerful Autism Inclusion Movement in India.

Mission

Our educational eco system has made great strides at mainstreaming. Today in India, we have special schools and regular inclusive schools. The emotional turmoil of the affected parents are alleviated till they reach 16 or 18. What Next is the big question that haunts them.

There is hardly any employment support available in India. Our mission is to work towards providing employability opportunities to individuals on the autism spectrum.

Value Proposition

Partner with enterprises to create neuro diversity inclusive workplaces, where boundaries are broken and everyone – neuro typical and atypical work together.
